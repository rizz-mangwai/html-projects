Raila Amolo Odinga

Baba • Agwambo • The People's President

His Remarkable Journey

  • 1945 — Born in Maseno, Kisumu County to Jaramogi Oginga Odinga.
  • 1970 — Graduated with a Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering from East Germany.
  • 1982–1988 — Detained without trial for six years.
  • 1992 — Elected Member of Parliament for Langata.
  • 2008–2013 — Served as the 2nd Prime Minister of Kenya.
  • 2018–2023 — African Union High Representative for Infrastructure Development.
  • 2025 — Passed away on 15th October at the age of 80.

Legacy

Raila Odinga, popularly known as Baba, was a towering figure in Kenyan politics for over four decades. He was a champion of multiparty democracy, constitutional reforms, and the voice of the ordinary Kenyan. He ran for the presidency five times and remained a symbol of resilience and hope.
